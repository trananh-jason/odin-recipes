Ingredients

  • 2 cups of warm water
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 2 large russet potatoes-peeled, and sliced into 1/4 strips
  • 6 cups vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt to taste

How to Make French Fries Step-by-Step

  1. Stir warm water with sugar in a medium bowl. Soak potatoes in water mixture for 15 minutes for 15 minutes. Remove potatoes from water and dry thoroughly on paper towels
  2. Heat oil in deep-fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  3. Cook potatoes in hot oil until golden, 5 to 6. Drain on paper towels and season with salt
